Play Bots

Play against personality-driven coach bots with legal move validation, opening prep, and Stockfish-backed analysis.

  • Legal board powered by chess.js
  • Stockfish 18 WASM plus fallback evaluator

Study

Bookup

A repertoire trainer built from public Chess.com archives, local study state, and source-aware Smart Theory trees.

  • Rapid, blitz, and bullet import paths
  • Repertoire, training, progress, and theory workspaces
  • Browser exports for PGN, JSON, and nested theory trees

Trace

Chess Connections

Trace proof-backed win chains from a Chess.com username to a master target with graph views, ledgers, and leaderboards.

  • Recorded rapid, blitz, and bullet wins only
  • Animated graph plus proof-game ledger
  • Cloudflare-backed search jobs and shared cache
